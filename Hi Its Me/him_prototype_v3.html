the v3 DNA
Rose + gold, not just amber
Rose (#E8608A) as the primary accent. Warm, saturated, unapologetic. Not pink-for-the-sake-of-it — it's intentional and it earns its place against the dark base.
Gradient as a signature
Rose to lavender to gold runs across every header bar, the logotype, the send button, the Pro badge, the away message accent. One consistent gradient that reads as the brand.
Nunito replaces Lato
Slightly rounded letterforms — warmer, more expressive, less corporate. Still clean and readable. The difference is subtle but it changes the feeling of every label and button.
Rose as the active state
Unread badges, active filters, selected chips, the send button, section labels — all rose. The color communicates "this is live, this matters" throughout.
Profile glow
Rose radial gradient wash behind the avatar on the profile screen. Subtle, warm, a little theatrical. The profile should feel like a moment, not a settings page.
What doesn't change
IBM Plex Mono for all screennames. No photos. AIM status dots with glow. The product's DNA is still AIM nostalgia — the rose layer adds the gay identity on top of it.
